Notes from the field

• The hellgrammite ate the minnow mayfly and multiple small winter stoneflies
• The hellgrammite ate other predators.
• The hellgrammite looks like a scorpion when it eats.

We were wondering if a predator would eat a predator. So we scooped the Hellgramite into a small plastic cup with water from the stream, and put a small winter stonefly into the cup with the Hellgramite. The Hellgramite gladly ate the little predator. So in conclusion, predators eat predators.
